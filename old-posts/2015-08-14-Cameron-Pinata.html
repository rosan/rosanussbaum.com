Collaborative Piñata made by member of the 2015 MFI Group for It's all Tropical's exhibiton/event/party Treat Yo Self at Bloc Projects, Sheffield. My contribution to the Piñata was a handmade chocolate cast of Prime Minister David Cameron's face.
