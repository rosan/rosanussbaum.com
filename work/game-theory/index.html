--- layout: project id: game-theory title: Even Lions Think this Way (Game Theory) ---

A Stepmania mod and training program designed to aid in the development of an intuitive grasp of game theory. Assisting artists everywhere in becoming comfortable with macro economics, evolutionary biology and neo-conservative politics.

Steps: Based on the games.

Song: Instrumentals computed algorithmically from games. MIDI generator developed in collaboration with Tom Wadley.

Vocals: based on game theory, finance and economics lectures from Harvard and Stanford and political speeches on austerity.

Video Instructional video. It will help you understand.
